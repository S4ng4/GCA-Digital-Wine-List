Brunello di Montalcino

Montalcino, Toscana

$89.99
Grape Variety Sangiovese Grosso
Vintage 2017
Alcohol 14.5%
Aging 4 years in oak

Brunello di Montalcino represents the pinnacle of Tuscan winemaking. This majestic expression of Sangiovese Grosso showcases intense aromas of dark cherry, leather, wild herbs, and spice.

Carefully aged for four years, including two in French oak barrels, this wine displays exceptional structure with firm yet refined tannins and a remarkably long, complex finish. With its impressive aging potential, this Brunello will continue to evolve beautifully for decades.

Tasting Notes

Appearance Deep ruby red with garnet reflections, excellent concentration
Nose Intense and complex with notes of black cherry, leather, tobacco, and violet
Palate Full-bodied with great structure, balanced acidity, and firm tannins
Finish Exceptionally long and persistent with notes of spice and dark fruit

About the Producer

Tenuta il Poggione

Tenuta il Poggione is one of the historic estates in the Brunello di Montalcino appellation, with winemaking traditions dating back to the late 19th century. Located on the southwestern side of Montalcino, their vineyards benefit from ideal sun exposure and mineral-rich soils. The estate practices sustainable viticulture and traditional winemaking methods, producing wines that authentically express the Montalcino terroir. Their Brunello is consistently ranked among the finest expressions of Sangiovese in Tuscany.
